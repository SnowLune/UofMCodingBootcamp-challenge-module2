About Me

I have always been naturally curious, and I approach my work with passion and a strong desire to be an essential part of a team. I'm eager to start my career in IT and I love to learn.

Computers have been a big part of my life ever since I was 5 years old and played around install a new operating system on the family computer. In my teens I became really interested in efficient software and operating systems. I installed my first Linux distribution when I was 13 on the same, now old, IBM computer I had tried to install Windows ME on when I was 5.

I became very interested in software and coding when I was 17 and started to learn JavaScript online. I've since gained a lot of knowledge and even more intrigue in all manner of programming and design, but especially use of low-level languages and language features.
